This week, after a break day on Monday, we focus on more complex rules for differentiation. Specifically, we look at the product and quotient rules, which allow us to calculate derivatives of \(f\cdot g\) and \(f/g\) in terms of the derivatives of \(f\) and \(g\text{.}\) Then, we apply these to look at derivatives of other trig functions in terms of the derivatives of \(\sin(x)\) and \(\cos(x)\text{.}\)
